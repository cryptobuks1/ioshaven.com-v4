How to Install?

1
Launch Rescue Server

Leave Rescue Server running in the background on your computer.

2
Connect to WiFi

Enable iTunes WiFi Sync and connect to the same WiFi as Rescue Server.

3
Download Apps

Browse and download apps directly from Rescue.

4
Apps Refresh Automatically

Apps are refreshed in the background when on same WiFi as Rescue Server.
